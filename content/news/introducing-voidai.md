# Introducing VoidAI: Your Partner for the Agentic Web

Today, we're introducing VoidAI—the intelligent chat interface that brings the power of the Agentic Web to everyone. VoidAI is your partner for life, providing seamless access to multi-model AI conversations while connecting you to the entire Voidnet ecosystem of tools and agents.

## What is VoidAI?

VoidAI is an advanced AI chat platform designed from the ground up for the agentic era. Unlike traditional chat interfaces, VoidAI understands that intelligence comes from connections—not just models. Through deep integration with Voidnet's marketplace, VoidAI can dynamically access thousands of tools, resources, and specialized agents to solve complex problems.

### Multi-Model Intelligence
VoidAI supports multiple AI providers simultaneously:
- **Client-side processing** with Puter for privacy-focused interactions
- **Server-side scaling** with OpenAI and other enterprise providers
- **Intelligent routing** that selects the best model for each task
- **Seamless switching** between providers without interrupting your workflow

### Agentic Workflows
Gone are the days of simple question-and-answer. VoidAI enables sophisticated multi-step workflows:
- **Tool chaining**: Automatically discover and use relevant tools from the Voidnet marketplace
- **Resource integration**: Pull in live data from connected services and APIs
- **Prompt orchestration**: Access specialized prompt templates for complex tasks
- **Task management**: Handle long-running operations with progress tracking
